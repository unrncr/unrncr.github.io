The Advisors

The advisors assist the team in project design. Each advisor plays a different role in helping the NCR project succeed.



Dr. Harris

Dr. Harris is a professor in the department of Computer Science and Engineering. He is involved in the University of Nevada, Reno's Brain Computation Lab, as well as the High Performance Computation and Visualization Lab. Dr. Harris helps to guide the team goals and project developments.



Devyani Tanna

Devyanni is a master's student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Nevada, Reno. She is dedicated to research in the field of computational neuroscience, with an interest in human computer interaction. Devyani advises the team on computational neuroscience topics and works on the brain model database design.



Dr. Dascalu

Dr. Dascalu is the professor advising the CS 426 Senior Projects in Computer Science class. He oversees the projects and guides the teams towards success. His emphasis is in Software Engineering and industry design processes.
